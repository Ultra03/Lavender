What is Miku-Chan?

Miku-Chan is a runs off of Discord.JS, an API wrapper made by fans of Discord. Miku-Chan was made by hardcore Discord lovers for hardcore Discord Lovers. So, it includes key features for running your server like purging commands, clearing the existence of a person on your server, filtering language, and more. And, it comes with fun features as well, such as music, lotteries, and voting.
