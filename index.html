What is Lavender?

Lavender is a Discord.JS bot made by hardcore Discord lovers for hardcore Discord lovers. So, it includes key features for running your server like purging commands, clearing the existence of a person on your server, filtering language, and more. And, it comes with fun features as well, such as music, lotteries, and voting.
